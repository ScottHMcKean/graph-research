# The Kestrel Drone

The Kestrel is an autonomous inspection drone produced by the Aerial Systems
division of Northwind Robotics. It was launched in 2019 and is the company's
flagship product.

The Kestrel runs on the Wingspan flight controller, a software platform also
developed by Northwind Robotics. Wingspan was written primarily by Marcus Webb's
team. The Kestrel carries the Hawkeye camera module, which is supplied by Opticore
Instruments, a partner company based in Edmonton.

Meridian Energy deploys the Kestrel to inspect its pipelines. The data collected by
the Kestrel feeds into Meridian Energy's maintenance program, which is managed by
Sofia Chen.
