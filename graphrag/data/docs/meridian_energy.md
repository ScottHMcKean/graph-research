# Meridian Energy

Meridian Energy is a pipeline operator based in Alberta. It is one of the largest
customers of Northwind Robotics. Meridian Energy is led by its Chief Executive
Officer, David Holt.

Meridian Energy runs a maintenance program that relies on autonomous inspection.
The program is managed by Sofia Chen, who reports to David Holt. The maintenance
program uses both the Kestrel drone and the Badger crawler from Northwind Robotics.

In 2022, Meridian Energy and Northwind Robotics signed a multi-year partnership to
co-develop inspection tooling. Opticore Instruments also supplies camera hardware to
Meridian Energy under a separate agreement.
