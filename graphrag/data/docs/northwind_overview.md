# Northwind Robotics Overview

Northwind Robotics is a robotics company headquartered in Calgary. It was founded
in 2014 by Dr. Lena Ortiz, who still serves as Chief Executive Officer. The company
designs autonomous inspection drones for the energy sector.

Northwind Robotics operates two divisions: Aerial Systems, led by Marcus Webb, and
Ground Systems, led by Priya Nair. The Aerial Systems division builds the Kestrel
drone, the company's flagship product. The Ground Systems division builds the Badger
crawler robot used for pipeline inspection.

Northwind Robotics partners with Meridian Energy, a large pipeline operator, which
uses the Kestrel drone across its Alberta operations.
